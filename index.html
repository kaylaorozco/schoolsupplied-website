Why this exists

"Somewhere around my third year of back-to-school supply lists, I realized I was solving the same problem every August: different lists from different teachers, overlapping items, three Target trips to get everything. Nothing existed that handled it well. So I'm building it."

— Kayla, creator of School Supplied and one overwhelmed parent
